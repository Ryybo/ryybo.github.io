My Film Projects

Explore my creative journey through a collection of compelling film projects that were stories of discovery, love and introspection.

Hindrance

Immersed in virtual reality to sculpt an ideal existence, a high school daughter remains oblivious to the impending intertwining of her meticulously crafted digital utopia with the challenges of her real-world existence.

Tinder Date

In a quest to slim down for love, a guy discovers that the key to his happiness wasn't in shedding pounds but in realizing his intrinsic worth.

Mirror Image

Haunted by a forgotten past, a man finds the shadows of his history returning to cast an unsettling presence in his present.
